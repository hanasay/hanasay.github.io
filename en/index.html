RVC Studio · AI Voice Conversion Platform

Upload a recording and let AI speak it — or sing it — in someone else's voice.

No technical knowledge required: log in, pick a voice model, drop in an audio file, and download the result. You can also type text and have the AI speak it, convert your voice live through a microphone, or clone an entirely new voice from just a few seconds of reference audio.

  • File-based voice conversion
  • Text-to-speech (TTS)
  • Real-time mic voice changing
  • Zero-shot voice cloning
  • Train your own voice models
  • Accounts & access control
